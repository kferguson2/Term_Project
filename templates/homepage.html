Hello! We are so excited to help you find courses!

As fellow Babson students, we know how time-consuming and difficult it can be to navigate across all the Babson websites to determine what courses you need to take to fulfill your requirements.
So we decided to create this tool that will help you find courses that are being offered during a specified semester!

There are three different search options available for your use:

1. Concentration Course Finder

The Concentration Course Finder will help you find courses that count towards your concentration requirments.

2. Course Type Finder

The Course Type Finder will allow you to find courses of a designated type, such as LVA, CVA, or HSS courses.

3. Course Level Finder

The Course Level Finder will help you find courses that will count towards your Advanced Liberal Arts Electives, Advanced Electives, or Free Electives credits.


Please navigate to your desired Course Finder to begin finding classes that are right for you!
